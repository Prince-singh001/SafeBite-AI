About SafeBite AI

AI Powered Food Freshness Detection

SafeBite AI is an intelligent food quality analysis system developed using Deep Learning, MobileNetV2, OpenCV and Image Processing technologies.

🎯 Problem Statement

Food spoilage causes health risks, food wastage and financial losses. Traditional inspection methods are slow and depend heavily on human observation.

💡 Proposed Solution

SafeBite AI uses image-based deep learning models to identify food items and determine whether they are fresh or spoiled.

🚀 Project Goal

To provide a fast, accurate and user-friendly food freshness detection platform accessible through a modern web application.
